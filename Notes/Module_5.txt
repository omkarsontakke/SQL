===========================Module 5: Aggregations & GROUP BY===================

What is Aggregation?
Ans:- Aggregation means reducing the number of rows into the single summary value.
    example: Total salary per department

Why GROUP BY Exists?
Ans:- If you want result per group, you must group first

Difference between WHERE and HAVING Clause?
Ans:- WHERE filter the rows before grouping
      HAVING Filter groups after aggregation

Does GROUP BY Sort the data?
Ans:- No, ORDER BY is required

COUNT(*) vs COUNT(column_name)
Ans:- COUNT(*) are count all values from the rows(Include null values)
      COUNT(column_name) count that specific column rows(Exclude null values)
